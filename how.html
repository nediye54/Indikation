How it works

MDG transforms a vague situation into decision-security: what is unstable, why, and what architecture fixes it.

What you get in minutes

A clear diagnosis

Primary instability + causal chain + the first leverage point that reduces uncertainty.

A concrete action architecture

Immediate stabilization moves + rebuild plan that prevents recurrence.

Process

1) One sentence

You describe the situation in one sentence. MDG uses it as the anchor for meaning and scope.

2) Decide intent

Stabilize = reduce tension fast. Rebuild = change structure so the same pattern cannot return.

3) Quick-scan

A few high-leverage axes (load, decisions, energy/error, growth/complexity, power-responsibility) create a structural fingerprint.

4) Precision questions

MDG asks only what increases precision. Each question is designed to reduce ambiguity, not to collect “more text”.

5) Output

You receive a concise assessment. If you generate the Executive Report, you get a premium, operational plan.

Private vs Business

Private

Language, examples and instructions are tailored to personal systems (roles, relationships, daily life, self-management).

Business

Business-token unlocks a separate question set and Executive Output (roles, cadence, KPIs, decision rights, operating model).

Privacy & data

Inputs are sent to the API only to generate the assessment/report. Do not include secrets or names you don’t want processed.
